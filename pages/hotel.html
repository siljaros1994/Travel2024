QAWRA Palace Resort & SPA

Overlooking Salina Bay,the hotel has its own indoor and outdoor swimming pools. It also features a mini golf course and a games room with pool table. Breakfast at the Qawra Palace is buffet style and includes both sweet and savoury items such as eggs, sausages and cereal. The restaurant serves international cuisine at lunch and dinner.

This 4-star hotel is 25 metres from the nearest bus stop and 700 metres from Bugibba centre. St. Julian's is 10 minutes' drive away.

Features

  • 3 swimming pools
  • Spa and wellness facilities
  • By the beach
  • Very good breakfast
